You are in the right place, We will give you the best chance at reinstating your account with the correct customized suspension appeal letter and plan of action (POA) for your situation.


# Main reasons for an Account suspension:-

1. Multiple accounts held by the same person.

2. Product listing is reported as a fake or counterfeit product.

3. Product listing violates Amazon's Marketplace Items Condition Guidelines. e.g. used items which are sold as new.

4. Safety complaints from customers.

5. High negative Customer Experience Rating (NCX).

6. Selling restricted/prohibited Items.

7. Order Defect Rate (ODR) is too high.

8. Other policy violations.

Ways to Prevent Suspension or Reinstatement

Have the Correct Documentation

Before you open your seller account, make sure you have all the necessary paperwork. Any discrepancies in your paperwork can result in a denial of your application or a suspension in the future.

Remove Any Problematic Products

Make a risk assessment and value judgment before you decide that you couldnâ€™t possibly consider deleting a favorite product. The busy season is not the time to be taking these risks, as it could come at a very costly price to your business after an already difficult year.

Double-Check Your Keywords

Itâ€™s quite common for sellers to go overboard when they list their products by overloading on the keywords they choose. This is costly for your Pay Per Click (PPC) campaigns.

Outsource Your Work That You canâ€™t Handle

With the global increase in Sellers, the competition on Amazon's platform is increasing. Thankfully, this is something you can anticipate. Employ the help of specialists in the areas that are most important to you, or to those where you lack the knowledge.

Be Proactive And Honest

Stay up-to-date with all policy changes. Be aware of what people are saying about your business and the reviews they are leaving, and be present on your Account. The best way to stay on top of things is to remain attentive and know exactly what is happening on a daily basis.

Web Design/Development

Web design and development are two distinct but closely related areas of web creation. Web design is the process of creating and designing a website. It involves using a variety of web technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create a visually appealing and user-friendly website. Web designers work with a variety of clients, from individuals and small businesses to large corporations, to create websites that meet their needs and goals.

Web development is the process of creating and maintaining websites. It involves using a variety of programming languages and technologies to build and implement the functionality of a website. The main languages used in web development are HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Web developers are responsible for creating the code that makes a website run, and for ensuring that the site is secure, fast and reliable.

Web design and development go hand in hand, as web developers take the design created by web designers and turn it into a functioning website. A web designer creates the layout and visual design of the website, while the web developer takes that design and builds the website using code. Together, web designers and developers work to create a cohesive and visually appealing user experience that is also functional and efficient.

A website is a crucial component of an e-commerce business as it serves as the main platform for customers to discover and purchase products or services, in addition to providing a secure and easy shopping experience.

Digital Marketing

Digital marketing is the use of digital channels, such as search engines, social media, email, and websites, to promote a product or service. It involves using various tactics, such as search engine optimization (SEO), content marketing, pay-per-click advertising (PPC), and social media marketing, to reach and engage with customers online.

The goal of digital marketing is to reach customers where they are spending time online and to use data and analytics to continually improve the effectiveness of marketing campaigns.

Digital Marketing Services.

Search Engine Optimization

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the practice of optimizing your website to improve its ranking in search engine results pages (SERPs). The goal of SEO is to increase the visibility and traffic of your website, by making it more visible and attractive to search engines. SEO is an ongoing process, and results may take time to appear.

Pay-Per-Click Advertising

PPC (Pay-Per-Click) is a type of online advertising model where advertisers pay a fee each time their ad is clicked. PPC advertising can be a highly effective way to drive traffic to your website and generate leads and sales.PPC advertising is a highly competitive space, and success requires ongoing effort and optimization. Set a goal, Before start a PPC campaign.

Social Media Marketing

It is the process of promoting a product, service or brand through various social media platforms. Social media marketing can include tactics such as creating a brand page, running ads, creating and sharing content, and engaging with customers on various social media platforms like Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, etc.

Content Marketing

Content marketing is a marketing strategy that involves creating and sharing valuable, relevant, and consistent content to attract and retain a clearly defined audience - with the goal of driving profitable customer action. It is a long-term strategy that requires ongoing effort and patience. Consistently creating and sharing high-quality content will help you build a loyal audience and drive profitable customer action over time.

Email Marketing

Email marketing is a type of digital marketing that involves sending promotional messages or advertisements to a group of people via email. The goal of email marketing is to build relationships with potential or existing customers, promote products or services, and ultimately drive sales and revenue. Email marketing campaigns can be targeted or mass emails, and can include a variety of content.

Affiliate Marketing

It is a type of performance-based marketing in which a business rewards one or more affiliates for each visitor or customer brought about by the affiliate's own marketing efforts. Affiliate marketing can be done through a variety of channels, such as websites, blogs, social media, email, and mobile apps. There are different types of affiliate marketing such as pay-per-sale, pay-per-click, and pay-per-lead.

Display Advertising

Display advertising is a form of online advertising where businesses place visual ads, such as banner ads, videos, and other types of multimedia, on websites and apps to reach a wider audience. One of the advantages of display advertising is the ability to target specific audiences and track the performance of the ads through metrics.

Mobile Marketing

Mobile marketing is the practice of marketing to consumers on their mobile devices, such as smartphones and tablets, through SMS, MMS, apps, and mobile websites. Mobile marketing can include tactics such as SMS and MMS text message marketing, mobile apps, mobile websites, QR codes, location-based marketing, and push notifications.

Video Marketing

Video marketing is the use of video content to promote a product or service and engage with customers. It involves creating and distributing video content, such as product demonstrations, explainer videos, live streams, and customer testimonials, on various platforms like YouTube, Moj , Instagram, Facebook    etc.

1. Trademark Registration...

Trademark registration is the process of obtaining legal protection for a trademark. A trademark is a distinctive symbol, design, or expression that identifies a product or service and distinguishes it from those of others. By registering a trademark, the owner can legally prevent others from using a similar mark for similar products or services, which can lead to consumer confusion.

The process of trademark registration typically involves conducting a trademark search, filing a trademark application with the relevant government agency, and potentially going through an examination and opposition process. Once the trademark is registered, the owner can use the Â® symbol to indicate that the trademark is legally protected.

4. Easier enforcement

5. Potential licensing and monetization opportunities

6. Use as collateral
